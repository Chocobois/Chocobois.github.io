Bullet hell through dawn and dusk.

**Twilight Wings** was made during [Global Game Jam 2022](https://globalgamejam.org/).

### Controls

* Keyboard
    * WASD or arrowkeys to move
    * Press [Space] to toggle between day and night
* Mobile
    * Touch and drag to move
    * Tap with a second finger to toggle between day and night
* Mouse
    * Click and drag to move
    * Right-click to toggle between day and night
